Water Saving Tips

    Water Conservation Strategies

    Rainwater Harvesting

    Collect and store rainwater in barrels or tanks for use during dry periods.

    Timed Watering

    Water early morning or late evening to reduce evaporation losses.

    Mulching

    Apply 5-10cm of organic mulch to retain soil moisture.

    Drought-Resistant Plants

    Choose native plants adapted to your climate's natural rainfall.
