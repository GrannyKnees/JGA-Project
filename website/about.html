Our Mission

Food security continues to be a major issue in South African communities, especially where families have limited access to fresh produce. In response to the crisis, community food gardens have become a popular and practical solution.

Our research focuses on the challenges and opportunities of food gardening in communities with specific attention on community gardens. These community gardens have potential environmental awareness, promoting food security and create local and ethnic identity.

Our Objectives

Research

Identify existing food gardens in various South African communities

Nutrition

Explore the role of food gardens in improving nutrition and reducing hunger

Documentation

Document gardening methods and best practices

Innovation

Propose ways to scale and improve food gardening initiatives
